Workshop

Indigenous Researchers

Unicamp, 27/11/2023

      The workshop will bring together indigenous graduate students and indigenous undergraduate students engaged in research projects at Unicamp and partner universities to discuss their experiences with research. What has it been like to be an indigenous researcher? How did you define your research topic? How has the relationship been with your supervisor and the research team? Do you seek to bridge any connections with indigenous knowledge or indigenous contexts (it's not mandatory!)? The idea is to provide an open space for conversation about the research experience, being an indigenous researcher.

      The conversation will include non-indigenous researchers who have been collaborating with indigenous communities: Laura Rival (Oxford University), Velina Ninkova (Oslo Metropolitan University), Ana Maria Rabelo Gomes (UFMG), Clarice Cohn (UFSCar), Joana Cabral (Unicamp), Chantal Medaets (Unicamp).
